Prabhas

" Uppalapati Venkata Suryanarayana Prabhas Raju (born 23 October 1979), known mononymously as Prabhas, is an Indian actor who works predominantly in Telugu cinema. One of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema Prabhas has featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list three times since 2015 based on his incom popularity.He has received seven Filmfare Awards South nominations and is a recipient of the Nandi Award and the SIIMA Award."
